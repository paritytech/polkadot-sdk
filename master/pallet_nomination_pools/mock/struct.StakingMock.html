pub fn slash_by(pool_id: PoolId, amount: Balance)

Mimics a slash towards a pool specified by pool_id. This reduces the bonded balance of a pool by amount and calls Pools::on_slash to enact changes in the nomination-pool pallet.

Does not modify any SubPools of the pool as Default::default is passed for slashed_unlocking.
