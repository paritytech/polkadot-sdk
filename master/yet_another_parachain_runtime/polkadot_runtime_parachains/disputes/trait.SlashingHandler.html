Punishment hooks for disputes.

Source

fn punish_for_invalid( session: u32, candidate_hash: CandidateHash, losers: impl IntoIterator<Item = ValidatorIndex>, backers: impl IntoIterator<Item = ValidatorIndex>, )

Punish a series of validators who were for an invalid parablock. This is expected to trigger a large punishment for backers and a medium punishment for other approvers.

Source

fn punish_against_valid( session: u32, candidate_hash: CandidateHash, losers: impl IntoIterator<Item = ValidatorIndex>, backers: impl IntoIterator<Item = ValidatorIndex>, )

Punish a series of validators who were against a valid parablock. This is expected to be a minor punishment.
