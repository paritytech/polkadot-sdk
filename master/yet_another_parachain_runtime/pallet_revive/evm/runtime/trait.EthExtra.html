where <Self::Config as Config>::Nonce: TryFrom<U256>, <Self::Config as Config>::RuntimeCall: SetWeightLimit,

Convert the unsigned crate::Call::eth_transact into a CheckedExtrinsic. and ensure that the fees from the Ethereum transaction correspond to the fees computed from the encoded_len and the injected weight_limit.

§Parameters
  • payload: The RLP-encoded Ethereum transaction.
  • encoded_len: The encoded length of the extrinsic.
